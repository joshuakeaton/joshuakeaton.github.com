My Mission Statement (by Joshua Keaton)

Hi, my name is Joshua Keaton and I put this site together to spread the good news: the best time to enjoy life is right now!

Living in the key of Jesus is taking life as it comes while walking (or biking!) around and having a great time. My goal is life is to enjoy my time here on this Earth and if I can help a few people enjoy their time too, well then that’s just dandy.

It doesn’t take money or cars and it doesn’t take a microwave or jewelery, nor does it take fancy meals or even a place to call your own; all it takes is a little courage, a lot of care, and a whole caboodle of walkin’ about! That’s living in the key of Jesus!

Praise!

Josh
